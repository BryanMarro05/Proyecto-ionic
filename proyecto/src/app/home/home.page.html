About Charlie Boy

Founded in San Salvador with a passion for authentic Italian flavors and American grill classics, Charlie Boy Pizza & Grill brings together the best of both worlds. Every pizza is handcrafted with love, using fresh local ingredients and our secret family recipe dough.

We believe in quality, tradition, and the joy of sharing a great meal with family and friends.
